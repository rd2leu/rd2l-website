About Us

RD2L EU is a Dota 2 league that is open to all players in Europe. We have a wide range of skill levels and players from all over Europe. We have a friendly and welcoming community that is always looking for new players to join us. If you are looking for a fun and competitive Dota 2 league, then RD2L EU is the place for you.

RD2L Schedule

Team Registration

Official registration opens with sign-up forms published on tournament forums. Players can register individually or apply to become team captains.

Availability Confirmation

Mandatory check-in period where registered players confirm their participation and availability for published tournament dates.

Captain Selection

Announcement of appointed team captains. Each captain receives a draft budget (coins) to build their roster through player auctions.

Live Draft Event

Official draft session where captains competitively bid on registered players. Highest bidder secures the player for their team.

Team Finalization

Publication of finalized team rosters. Teams receive their initial match schedule and preparation period begins.

Championship Phase

Teams compete in round-robin qualifiers followed by elimination playoffs. Final champions receive exclusive Discord roles and tournament trophies.

Rules

RD2L EU has a set of rules that all players must follow. These rules are in place to ensure that all players have a fair and enjoyable experience. Some of the key rules include:

  • All players must be respectful to each other
  • Players must follow the schedule and show up for matches on time
  • Players must follow the draft process and play in the position assigned to them
  • Players must not use any cheats or hacks

These are just a few of the rules that all players must follow. For a full list of rules, please visit our rules page.
